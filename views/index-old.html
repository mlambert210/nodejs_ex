Home phone

Cogeco Home Phone is a residential service and must only be used for residential purposes. The minutes included in each long distance plan offered are calculated on a calendar month basis. If the number of minutes included in the chosen long distance plan is reached within a given month, the additional minutes will be charged in extra at the then current rate, per minute per country, applicable for the remaining of the month. International Long distance plans are conditional to a subscription to one of our Home Phone packages. After the promotional period of 3 months, the then current standard rate will apply. To qualify for this offer, you must subscribe to 2 service(s), including Cogeco Digital Cable TV or Cogeco High Speed Internet.
